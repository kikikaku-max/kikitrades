Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

KikiTrades ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our TikTok content publishing application ("KikiTrades Publisher").

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize KikiTrades Publisher to access your TikTok account, we receive the following information through TikTok's API:

- Basic profile information (open ID, display name, avatar) via user.info.basic scope
- Permission to upload and publish content via video.upload scope

3. How We Use Information

We use the authorized access solely to publish Forex education content (photos, videos, and text) to TikTok on behalf of the authenticated creator account. We do not use your information for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage

We store only the access tokens necessary to publish content. These tokens are stored securely on our local systems and are not transmitted to any third-party servers. We do not store any personal information from your TikTok profile beyond what is needed for authentication.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your TikTok data is used exclusively for the content publishing functionality described above.

6. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary for the publishing service to function. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings, which will immediately terminate our ability to publish content.

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your access tokens and any associated data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to revoke KikiTrades Publisher's access to your TikTok account at any time. You may also contact us to request deletion of any stored data.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
